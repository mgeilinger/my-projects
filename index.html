My Projects

  • London Detective
    A Sherlock Holmes style murder mystery detective game designed using Javascript, HTML and CSS. The player travels around different areas of London, speaking to people and gathering clues relating to case you are trying to solve. Once you think you have enough information to solve the case, you must travel to Scotland Yard in Westminster, and you are able to submit your answer.
  • Wildflower Calendar
    A website providing information about flowering plants for each month in the UK. This was designed as a tool to give a general idea of what plants to look out for throughout the year.
